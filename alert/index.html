Privacy Notice

Speech Recognition: This app uses your browser's built-in speech recognition, which may send audio to external services (like Google or Apple) for processing. The app itself does not collect or send any data to its own servers. Using `whisper.cpp` client-side is not feasible for web apps, but a server-based implementation could be a future alternative.

Data Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. Nothing is sent to any server. Use the export feature to backup your data.

Usage Tips

  • Speak naturally: "Client from web development 69k rupees"
  • Tags are extracted automatically from your text
  • Tasks checked off are auto-logged to your diary journal
  • Click calendar days to view entries for that date
